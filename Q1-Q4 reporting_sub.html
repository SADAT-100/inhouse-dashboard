Quarterly Report Submission

Marketplace Intelligence Portal — Regal Inhouse & BackComfort

Instructions: No rigid template is required. Upload all raw data files, platform exports, or settlement reports currently available for your channel (Excel, CSV, PDF, ERP downloads). Our analytics team will extract and organize all necessary metrics.
🔴 Mandatory Requirements
  • Sales Performance: Revenue, Orders, Units Sold, AOV, Best Seller Product/Category.
  • Monthly Summary: Revenue, Orders, Net Profit, and Margin breakdown per month.
  • Payment Status: Expected, Received, Outstanding, and Overdue amounts.
🟠 High Priority (P&L & Orders)
  • P&L Breakdown: Revenue, Discounts, COGS, Commissions, Shipping, Ad Spend, Gross/Net Profit.
  • Order Fulfillment: Delivered, Pending, Cancelled, Returned Orders + Return Rate %.
🔵 Optional / Channel Data
  • Logistics: Avg Delivery Days, Late Deliveries Count, Shipping Issues.
  • Advertising: Ad Spend, Attributed Sales, ROAS / ACOS.
  • Inventory: Out-of-Stock SKUs, Slow-Moving Stock, Unsellable Inventory.
🟢 Business Highlights
  • Achievements: Key milestones reached.
  • Challenges: Operational or commercial bottlenecks.
  • Action Plan: Opportunities & recommendations for next quarter.
